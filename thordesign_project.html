This company makes bespoke clothing and leather goods. The needs were to redesign and simplify the logo to be more easily scalable and recognizable. The style concept of the company is classic, luxury, unique, of which I used to guide my design decisions. I used Adobe Illustrator, PhotoShop and InDesign for this project.
