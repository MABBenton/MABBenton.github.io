Melissa A Benton

My design philosophy is simplicity and functionality, sprinkled with an organic aesthetic. I strive to create websites that are functional for users of all accessibility needs and with a clear flow achieved through minimalist design. When working with a design concept I look to nature for inspiration for utilizing colors and shapes effectively to create the desired emotional response of a website, interior space or artwork piece. When these three elements are used in conjunction, the wow factor can be achieved. Design should add something positive to the human experience.
