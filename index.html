My design philosophy is simplicity and functionality, enhanced by an organic aesthetic. I create websites that are functional for users at all levels of accessibility with clear flow achieved by minimalist design. About Me

My life has been a web of varied experiences, many failures and many lessons learned. I have a BA in Interior Design and Cultural Studies. Through my studies I learned various design elements, the design process, analyzing user experience, group collaboration, understanding and drawing up layers of interconnected systems, all of which are transferable skills to web development. Having worked in sales and as a stewardess/deckhand on mega-yachts I learned the importance of communication, being able to work as a team and individually to achieve a shared goal. In design, I excel at utilizing color, typography and spatial awareness to create visual intrigue, with a keen eye on the details. My creativity expands beyond interior design and architecture to furniture design, upholstery, jewelry making, sketching, painting and print work. I'm passionate and curious about whole body health and connecting to our environment. I love to travel the world while pursuing my other passions, which include rock climbing, snowboarding, yoga, swimming, sailing and scuba diving, to name a few. My varied work experiences, travels and interests play a part in how I approach the design process and arrive at design solutions.
