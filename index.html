My design philosophy is simplicity and functionality, enhanced by an organic aesthetic. ABOUT

About Me:

My life has been a web of varied experiences, many failures and many lessons learned. I have a BA in Interior Design and Cultural Studies. Through my studies I learned various design elements, the design process, analyzing user experience, understanding and drawing up layers of interconnected systems and group collaboration, all of which are transferable skills to web development. Having worked in sales and as a stewardess/deckhand on mega-yachts I learned the importance of communication, being able to work as a team and individually to achieve a shared goal. In design, I excel at utilizing color, typography and spatial awareness to create visual intrigue and organization amongst information, with a keen eye on the details. My creativity expands beyond interior design and architecture to furniture design and reupholstery, jewelry making, sketching, painting and print work. I'm passionate and curious about whole body health and connection to our environment, of which I fulfill through world travel. The locations I choose to explore incorporate my other passions, which are rock climbing, snowboarding, yoga, swimming and scuba diving, to name a few. My varied work experiences, travels and interests play a part in how I approach the design process and arrive at design solutions.
